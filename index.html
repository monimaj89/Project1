About me

Welcome to my website. My name is Natalia and I am 36 years old. In 2013, I graduated from MA studies (literary culture) at the University of Lodz and, although I love books, cats and painting will always come first and second. I have been painting since I was a child, but my adventure with cats began a few years ago, after I took Kikko, my first cat, under my roof. It was love at first sight. This is how my passion and cat madness began. Blind Io, my other cat, came home less than a year later, and by then I was completely out of my mind. I love painting cats. Their elegance, calmness and individualism are an inexhaustible source of inspiration for me. In my paintings, I like to combine the art nouveau style with a bit of self-developed aesthetics. I am inspired by the works of Gustav Klimt, Japanese watercolors and... cats themselves of course! Please, make yourself comfortable, view my work and contact me if you need one of my paintings in your life.
